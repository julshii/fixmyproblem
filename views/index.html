HOW IT WORKS


POST!


Post a problem and add a couple of answers you just can't decide on. Others will pick whatever choice they think is best, and might even comment some answers you haven't thought of!

VOTE!


Vote on the answer you think is the best fix. If it's not there, write one for them! Your solutions' star-rating will determine your own. Get 5-stars, and your choices will be highly valued!

REPEAT!


After you answer a problem, you'll be given a new one to help fix! You can also look for problems of specific categories by typing their topic in the search bar! Happy fixing!
